Oxygen
======

Range of validity:

- Temperature: `54 - 300 K`
- Pressure: to `80 MPa`

Computation of thermodynamic properties is based on :cite:t:`SCHMIDT1985175` and :cite:t:`10.1063/1.555897`.
Viscosity and thermal conductivity is based on :cite:t:`lemmon2004viscosity`.
