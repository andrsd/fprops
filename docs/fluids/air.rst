Air
===

Range of validity:

- Temperature: `60 - 2000 K`
- Pressure: to `2000 MPa`

Computation of thermodynamic properties is based on :cite:t:`lemmon2000thermodynamic`.
Viscosity and thermal conductivity is based on :cite:t:`lemmon2004viscosity`.
